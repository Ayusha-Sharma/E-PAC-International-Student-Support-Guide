Peer Academic Coaching

Peer Academic Coaching supports students in developing effective academic strategies, goal-setting skills, and confidence to succeed.

What is Peer Academic Coaching?

Peer Academic Coaches assist students in becoming independent, self-assured learners. Through ongoing conversations, a Peer Coach can help you develop and implement strategies to achieve your academic goals.

Coaches help students achieve success by providing structure, study-skills building, and accountability. Current WSU students are eligible for services at no cost.

How PAC Can Support You:

  • Tips for New/Incoming Students
    New to campus? Need help finding resources? Let an experienced student set you on the right path.
  • Time Management and Planning
    Learn practical systems to break tasks into manageable steps and prioritize effectively.
  • Effective Study Strategies
    Discover proven study methods that help you meet your academic goals.
  • Improving and Maintaining Motivation
    Learn techniques to stay focused and motivated throughout the semester.
  • Effective Concentration
    Develop strategies to improve focus while studying and learning.
  • Planning for Tests and Final Exams
    Break down exam preparation into clear, manageable steps.
