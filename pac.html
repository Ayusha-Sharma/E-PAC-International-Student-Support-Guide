Peer Academic Coaching

Personalized academic support to help you build confidence, strengthen study strategies, and achieve your goals at WSU.

What is Peer Academic Coaching?

Peer Academic Coaches assist students in becoming independent and self-assured learners. Through ongoing conversations, coaches help you develop strategies, stay accountable, and work toward your academic goals.

Services are completely free for current WSU students and focus on skill-building, structure, and long-term academic success.
