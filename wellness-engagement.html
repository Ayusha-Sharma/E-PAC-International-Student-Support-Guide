Wellness & Campus Connection

Success is not only about academics — emotional support and belonging matter too.

🌿 Counseling & Wellness Center

Adjusting to a new academic system, culture, and environment can feel overwhelming. Stress, homesickness, anxiety, and burnout are common — even when classes are going well.

The Counseling & Wellness Center provides confidential support for emotional well-being, academic stress, and life transitions. Seeking support is a healthy and proactive step.

Services Include:

  • Individual counseling sessions
  • Group support and workshops
  • Stress management & coping strategies
  • Walk-in and crisis support

Location: 053 Student Union

🤝 Engagement & Campus Involvement

Engagement beyond the classroom plays an important role in student success. Feeling connected improves motivation, confidence, and well-being — especially for international students.

Engagement does not mean doing everything at once. Exploring opportunities at your own pace is encouraged.

Ways to Get Involved:

  • Find campus events and workshops
  • Explore student organizations
  • Track involvement through Engage
  • Discover leadership and volunteer opportunities
