# International Student Academic Support Guide

A web-based academic support guide designed to help international students better understand U.S. college systems, expectations, and available support resources.

This project was created as an independent initiative to provide clear, practical, and accessible information tailored specifically to international students navigating academic life in the United States.

---

## 🌎 Project Purpose

International students often face unique challenges such as:

- Understanding academic expectations
- Navigating campus resources
- Adjusting to new classroom environments
- Managing academic stress
- Finding appropriate support services

This website provides a structured and easy-to-navigate guide to address these areas.
